About Us

The Medical Center "EXCELLENCE" is a multifunctional institution of diagnosis, primary and specialized medical care. We offer our clients complete medical services, at a high quality standard using the most advanced medical diagnostic and laboratory equipment. The partial integration of the Medical Center into the existing health insurance system in the Republic of Moldova allows us to offer all the facilities related to the status of a contributor to the National Health Insurance Company. The Medical Center "EXCELLENCE" meets the modern needs of fast and efficient diagnosis, offering high-performance assessments and ensuring medical services at European standards, provided by competent medical personnel.
